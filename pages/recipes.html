Classic Gingerbread Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1½ tsp ginger
  • 1½ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup molasses

Instructions:

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Cream butter and sugar
  3. Combine and chill dough
  4. Roll out and cut shapes
  5. Bake at 350°F for 10 minutes

Hot Chocolate

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Marshmallows

Instructions:

  1. Heat milk until steaming
  2. Whisk in cocoa and sugar
  3. Add vanilla extract
  4. Top with marshmallows
