Holiday Recipes

Classic Gingerbread Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1½ tsp ginger
  • 1½ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup molasses

Instructions:

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Cream butter and sugar
  3. Combine all ingredients
  4. Chill dough
  5. Roll and cut shapes
  6. Bake at 350°F for 10 minutes

Hot Chocolate

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 oz dark chocolate
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Whipped cream
  • Marshmallows

Instructions:

  1. Heat milk until steaming
  2. Add chocolate and cocoa
  3. Whisk until smooth
  4. Add sugar to taste
  5. Top with cream and marshmallows

Christmas Sugar Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1½ cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • Royal icing

Instructions:

  1. Cream butter and sugar
  2. Add eggs and vanilla
  3. Mix in flour
  4. Chill dough
  5. Roll and cut
  6. Bake and decorate
